HTML is the structure and content of the web.

HTML Tags

Wrap text in 'tags' to denote its structure.

<h1>The main heading on the page</h1>
<p>Paragraph text</p>
<strong>Bold, alerting text</strong>
<em>Italic, emphasised text</em>.

Layout tags

<div>This is a divided piece of content</div>

<header>This is a header!</header>

<footer>This is a footer!</footer>
